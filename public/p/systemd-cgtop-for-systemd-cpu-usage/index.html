Q: How do I get % CPU usage of individual service in systemd?

A: Use `systemd-cgtop`
2023-07-19 · 2 min · 243 words · Yosua

TIL about systemd-cgtop from here when trying to debug tailscaled high CPU usage.

systemd-cgtop is interactive, but using:

systemd-cgtop -c

will sort the “control groups” by CPU usage.
